Sita Rama Kalyanam

"The Sacred Wedding of Sita & Rama — A Celestial Celebration"

Sita Rama Kalyanam is the most sacred and joyous occasion in our cultural calendar — a grand re-enactment of the divine wedding of Goddess Sita and Lord Rama as described in the Valmiki Ramayana. The Culture Club of Sri Chandrashekarendra Saraswati Vishwa Mahavidhyalaya is proud to host this blessed event, bringing together the entire university community in devotion, artistry, and celebration. The ceremony unfolds with Vedic rituals, classical music, devotional dance, and traditional decor — creating an atmosphere of divine grace and festivity.

🕉 Ram Navami Special
🪔
Vedic Rituals
Authentic Vivah rituals performed by learned Vedic priests, including Saptapadi, Mangalsutra, and Kanyadaan ceremonies.
🎶
Devotional Music
Live renditions of Ramayana verses, mangal geet, and classical Carnatic compositions by the university's culture club.
💃
Kolatam
A rythimic folk dance performances depicting scenes from the Ramayana — from Swayamvara to the divine wedding.
🌸
Floral Decor
Elaborate traditional decoration with marigold, jasmine and lotus — transforming the auditorium into a celestial mandap.
🍚
Prasadam Seva
Traditional prasadam distribution including panchamritam, pongal and sweet rice blessed during the ceremony for all attendees.
📸
Photography & Exhibit
Photo documentation and a special exhibit showcasing cultural heritage and previous years' celebrations of this sacred event.
